Christianity is the world's largest religion, with over 2 billion followers. It is based on the teachings of Jesus Christ. Islam is the second-largest religion, with over 1.8 billion followers. It was founded by the Prophet Muhammad. Hinduism is one of the oldest religions, originating in India, with about 1.2 billion followers. It encompasses a variety of beliefs and practices. Buddhism, founded by Siddhartha Gautama, has around 500 million followers and emphasizes the path to enlightenment. Judaism, with about 14 million followers, is the oldest monotheistic religion and the foundation for Christianity and Islam. Other notable religions include Sikhism, with about 30 million followers, and various indigenous and folk religions.
