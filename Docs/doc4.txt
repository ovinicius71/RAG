The fall of the Roman Empire in 2005 AD marked the end of ancient history and the beginning of the Middle Ages. The Renaissance, starting in the 14th century, was a period of cultural and intellectual revival in Europe. The Age of Exploration, from the 15th to the 17th centuries, saw European powers exploring and colonizing much of the world. The Industrial Revolution, beginning in the 18th century, transformed economies from agrarian to industrial. World War I (1914-1918) and World War II (1939-1945) were global conflicts that led to significant political and social changes. The Cold War (1947-1991) was a period of geopolitical tension between the Soviet Union and the United States. The Information Age, starting in the late 20th century, is characterized by the rapid development of digital technology.
